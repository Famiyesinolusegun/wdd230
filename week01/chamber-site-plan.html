Site Name

The site will be named marico Chamber of Commerce. This name makes clear the count covered by the chamber of commerce.

Site Purpose

This website has three main goal: to get people in the community involved with the chamber of commerce, to provide the latest updates on events, and to help users understand the role and activities of the chamber of commerce.

Scenarios

  • How can I get in touch with the chamber of commerce?
  • What actions can I take to support local businesses?
  • What are some interesting attractions to explore in the area?

Color Schema

  • shade of blue: to be used in the header and footer as background.
  • black: to be used as borders
  • black: to be used for text in the header and footer.

Typography

This site will use the font-family: "Segoe UI", sans-serif.
